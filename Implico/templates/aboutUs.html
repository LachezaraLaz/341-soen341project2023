About Us

The Vision

Finding a job can be a challenge, but finding skilled workers is another one! It is especially challenging to market yourself as a job applicant fresh out of university with no experience. How do you show off your accumulated skills and how can employers find up and coming talents within the industry? We founded Implico to bridge the gap between in the job market. Our main purpose is to streamline the hiring process. We have allowed skilled workers to put themselves and their work on display, as well as create a platform to browse for jobs and apply at the click of a button! Employers get the chance to upload their job postings and view candidates applying to their postings all on one platform! Let's revolutionize job searching and start hiring today!
